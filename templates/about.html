Our Story

Message Board is a modern, user-friendly platform designed to bring people together through simple, meaningful conversations.

Built with cutting-edge web technologies including Django, Bootstrap 5, and modern JavaScript, our platform offers a seamless experience for users to create, edit, and share their messages with the community.

Features

  • Create and share posts instantly
  • Edit and delete your own messages
  • User authentication and profiles
  • Real-time AJAX updates
  • Beautiful, responsive design
  • Toast notifications for actions

Technology Stack

Django 5.x Python 3.x Bootstrap 5 JavaScript SQLite AJAX

Frequently Asked Questions

To create a post:

  1. Make sure you're logged in
  2. Navigate to the home page
  3. Find the "Share your thoughts" text area
  4. Type your message
  5. Click the "Post" button

Your post will appear instantly at the top of the feed!

Yes! You have full control over your posts.

To edit: Click the pencil icon next to your post, make changes, and save.

To delete: Click the trash icon next to your post and confirm deletion.

Note: You can only edit or delete your own posts.

Creating an account is easy:

  1. Click the "Sign up" button in the navigation bar
  2. Fill in your desired username and password
  3. Click "Sign up" to create your account
  4. You'll be automatically logged in

The Pro badge indicates active users who regularly engage with the community.

All registered users receive this badge to show they're part of our growing community!

You have two options to logout:

  1. Click the "Logout" button in the top navigation bar
  2. Click your profile avatar, then select "Logout" from the dropdown menu

Yes! We take security seriously:

  • All passwords are encrypted
  • CSRF protection on all forms
  • Secure authentication system
  • User-specific post permissions

Quick Tips

  • Posts are paginated - use the page numbers at the bottom to view more posts
  • Hover over posts to see edit and delete options
  • Your profile avatar shows the first letter of your username
  • Toast notifications will confirm your actions
  • All changes happen instantly with AJAX - no page reloads needed!
